Jones Wellness and Advocate Center is dedicated to the memory of all the loved ones we have lost due to the lack of proper recovery resources. We are committed to ensuring that no one is left behind by providing comprehensive care and support for individuals facing addiction. As passionate advocates for disability rights, we strive to create an inclusive space where everyone, regardless of their circumstances, has access to the care they deserve. Our mission is to offer compassionate, holistic services that address both physical and mental health, empowering individuals and families to heal, recover, and thrive. Our services include addiction recovery support, behavioral health management, advocacy for disability rights, and resources to help individuals reintegrate into their communities with confidence and stability. Together, we are building a community where no one falls through the cracks, and everyone has the opportunity for a brighter future.
